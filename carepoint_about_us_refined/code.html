Our Story

Built by Healthcare Experts, for Healthcare Providers.

At CarePoint, we understand that practicing medicine is complex enough without the added burden of fighting for every dollar you've earned. We were founded on a simple principle: providers should focus entirely on patient care, while we focus entirely on their financial health.

Our team of certified medical coders, billing specialists, and dedicated healthcare administrators act as a seamless extension of your clinic. By leveraging pristine workflows and relentless follow-up, we consistently deliver 98%+ clean claim rates and drastically reduce days in A/R.
