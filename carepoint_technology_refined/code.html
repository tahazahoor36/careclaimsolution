Our Process

The CarePoint Revenue Workflow

A meticulously designed, technology-driven process that eliminates friction at every step of the patient journey.

assignment_ind

1. Patient Registration

Front-office integration securely captures accurate demographics and insurance data directly from your clinic's intake forms.

health_and_safety

2. Eligibility Verification

Automated API pings to payer databases instantly confirm active coverage, preventing downstream eligibility denials.

format_list_numbered

3. Medical Coding

Certified coders leverage advanced software to translate clinical encounters into precise ICD-10 and CPT codesets, ensuring maximum RVU capture.

fact_check

4. Claim Scrubbing

Claims are algorithmically audited against thousands of NCCI edits and payer-specific policies to eliminate formatting errors.

send

5. Claim Submission

Perfectly scrubbed claims are transmitted electronically via our secure clearinghouse network, reaching the payer within hours.

account_balance_wallet

6. Payment Posting

ERAs (Electronic Remittance Advices) are automatically ingested and reconciled, ensuring your bank deposits match your accounting ledgers exactly.

gavel

7. Denial Management

Any rejected claims are immediately flagged and routed to specialized appeals teams for rapid correction and resubmission.
