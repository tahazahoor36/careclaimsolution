Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions before scheduling your consultation.

What happens during the free consultation? expand_more

During our 30-minute consultation, a senior revenue cycle expert will review your current billing workflows, identify potential areas of revenue leakage, and explain exactly how our services can integrate with your practice to improve cash flow.

Do I have to change my current EHR? expand_more

No. We are software-agnostic and will seamlessly integrate our billing teams directly into your existing Electronic Health Record (EHR) system, whether you use Epic, Cerner, Athenahealth, AdvancedMD, or any other major platform.

How quickly can you onboard my practice? expand_more

Our typical onboarding process takes between 14 to 30 days depending on the size of your practice and the complexity of your current credentialing status. We handle the entire transition to ensure zero disruption to your cash flow.
